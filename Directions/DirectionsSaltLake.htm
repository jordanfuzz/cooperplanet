• Traveling North on I-15, take the 2nd Layton exit, exit 334.
  • Turn right (East) off the exit.
  • Then take your first right (at the light) on Gordon Ave.
  • Gordon Ave will curve back to the East so you will be headed East again.
  • You will see an LDS Church on the left about 3-4 blocks down Gordon.
  • Pass the church and then take the 3rd left after the church.
